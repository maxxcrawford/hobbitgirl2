THE MOVIES

Quick Review

  • The Film Trilogy. Three films were made of The Lord of the Rings, representing each book: The Fellowship of the Ring, The Two Towers, Return of the King. Return of the King will be released December 17th, 2003.
  • Director. The films were being directed by New Zealander Peter Jackson, who previously directed The Frighteners and Heavenly Creatures. He owns the digital imaging studio WETA and the film company ThreeFootSix.
  • Characters. A mixture of known and unknown actors is being used in all the roles.
  • Locations. The three films were made entirely in New Zealand. See 'Locations' page to find out why.
  • Producers. The films are a joint production by New Line Cinema and ThreeFootSix.
